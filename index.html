THE JINX BREAKER (PART 1)

I have always been in a group of people where new things started, where the ugly stopped and a new phase began.

My first year in university was spent in the Nsukka Campus of the University of Nigeria. After the first year, we were to move to a different campus in Enugu known as University of Nigeria Enugu Campus(UNEC). We looked forward to 'crossing over' to UNEC, it was an achievement, because it was an upward movement not just associated with geographical change, but it shows you have successfully completed all first year courses without any failure. Only those who have passed all their courses could crossover

We heard so many stories about UNEC, the good and Ugly. One of the ugly stories we heard was that two students shared a bed space in the hostel in UNEC.
